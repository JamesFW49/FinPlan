# FinPlan — Shareable Template

This is a separate, blank copy of FinPlan meant for sharing with other
people. It is completely independent from any other deployment of this
app — different hosting, different sync database, no connection
whatsoever to anyone else's data.

## Setting this up as your own deployment

1. Unzip this folder.
2. Go to **app.netlify.com/drop** and drag the unzipped folder onto the page (no account needed for a one-off link, or sign up free for a permanent one).
3. Open the link it gives you on your phone in Safari, tap Share → Add to Home Screen.

That's it — you now have your own independent copy of FinPlan with its own
data, its own device sync (if you choose to use it), and its own app lock.
Nothing you enter here is visible to, or connected with, whoever gave you
this file.

## Everything else

This is the full app — money tracker, debt tracker, investment tracker,
live forecasts, monthly tracking, device sync, and app lock. See the full
feature list and instructions by asking whoever shared this with you, or
refer to the general FinPlan documentation if you have it.

Your data is stored only on your own device(s) unless you turn on device
sync with a code of your own choosing — and even then, it's stored under a
separate database from any other FinPlan deployment, fully isolated.
